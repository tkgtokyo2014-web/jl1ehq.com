How far can an amateur radio station evolve?

## Body and Memory

My station stands on two foundations.

- **The body (remote operation)** — a 200W base station in Oiso, Kanagawa, operated from Nihonbashi, Tokyo over VPN. The IC-7760's detachable-controller architecture made this possible.
- **The memory (cloud logging)** — Wavelog on a self-built cloud server manages every QSO as the single source of truth, feeding LoTW, eQSL and QRZ.com directly.

## The Brain

On top of these comes a team of AI agents — DX hunting, propagation forecasting, award tracking, station health, contest strategy and QSO analytics — coordinated by a Chief Cyber Operator.

## Humans at the Center

AI organizes, analyzes and proposes. The final decision — and every QSO — always belongs to the human operator.

**Humans play the leading role; AI is the ultimate partner that expands human potential.**
